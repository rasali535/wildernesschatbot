# Setswana Language Integration - Quick Reference

## How to Use

### For Users

**Switch to Setswana:**

- Type: `Setswana`, `Tswana`, or `tn`

**Switch to English:**

- Type: `English` or `en`

**Auto-Detection:**

- Use Setswana words like: `dumela`, `dumelang`, `ke batla`, `thusa`
- The chatbot will automatically respond in Setswana

### Common Phrases

| English | Setswana |
|---------|----------|
| Hello | Dumela / Dumelang |
| I want to see big cats | Ke batla go bona dikatse tse dikgolo |
| Can you help me? | A o ka nthusa? |
| Thank you | Ke a leboga |
| Tell me about camps | Mpolelele ka dikhampo |
| I'm interested in... | Ke kgatlhegile ka... |

## Why Setswana?

- **33% of camps** are in Botswana (5 out of 15)
- **National language** of Botswana (~90% speak it)
- **Market expansion** to local African travelers
- **Cultural respect** and community alignment
